About us

Cub|e|vo was born out of the desire to help companies work more efficiently by concentrating on the core business, rather than managing laborious, repetitive and time-consuming activities. The tools we create will take care of that.

Although a young company, we bring to the table more than 12 years diverse-industry of experience. And we've also built some interesting stuff too. Data pipelines and automation flows for pharmaceutical clients, shared services cloud platform used in IT consultancy and web crawling tools for the tourism industry would be a few examples.

One more thing about us: we value our clients! They are our business partners and our goal is to establish long-lasting an mutually-beneficial relationships. We value their time and their work and we put extra effort into understanding what makes their world move, so we can provide the best service.
